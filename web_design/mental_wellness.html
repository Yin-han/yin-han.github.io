Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental wellness?

Mental wellness refers to your overall mental health and well-being.

How can therapy help me?

Therapy provides a safe space to talk about your feelings and work through challenges.

How do I book a session?

You can easily book a session by clicking on the "Get Matched" button below.

How much does therapy cost?

The cost of therapy varies depending on the type of service and the therapist you choose.

Who are the therapists?

Our therapists are licensed professionals specializing in various mental health issues.

Can therapy help with anxiety?

Yes, therapy is effective in helping individuals manage and reduce anxiety symptoms.
